Epipremnum aureum Varieties

This print features twelve watercolor illustrations of foliage from different varieties of Epipremnum aureum, commonly known as "Pothos". Originally from the tropics of Southeast Asia and Oceania, Epipremnum aureum is a species in the aroid family. The genera Pothos is in fact a separate group of aroids, and that name is no longer botanically correct for this species and its many varieties; however the shorter, easier to remember name "Pothos" seems to have stuck. These are some of the most popular and beloved tropical houseplants grown all over the world.
